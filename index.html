About Us-AGRADO

Background-The previous Name was Advanced Green Rural Initiative Development (AGRID) and The current name is Advanced Green Rehabilitation and Development Organization ( AGRADO ) is a boardâ€“led Organization a registered as Local Non Governmental, Non-Political and non-religious organization that has been working in 5 selected intervention regions of Democratic Republic Ethiopia included Amhara Regional states ,in partnership with both local and international Partners on poverty reduction, climate change ,food security, community health, emergencies and disasters response, Promoting peace and justice and development and human rights advocacy. Advanced Green Rehabilitation and Development Organization / AGRADO / is was established by voluntary and committed individuals person and registered as an Ethiopian Residents Charity in 2014 and again re- registered and accorded legal personality with Registry Number 3344 as a local None Governmental organization by proclamation Number 1113/2019.



Thus, AGRADO started its implementation by initiating and promoting the purpose and core activities of its engagement as an organization. AGRADO has six major implementation thematic areas which include Environment rehabilitation & mitigation adaptation to climate change, Food security and value chain, Community health through hygiene and sanitation, Youth engagement and capacity building, Gender equality through Women and girls Empowerment and other cross-cutting issues. AGRADO has a clear financial and transparent financial and admin manual for its project implementation. The financial transaction audited annually and approved by the board of directors. AGRADO has strong and committed board of directors to support and implement the organization towards its vision. AGRADO has draw backs on absence of HIV/AIDS work place policy still yet. .
